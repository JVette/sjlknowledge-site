About This

This website has been created for people involved, or want to become involved, in the "Fight for What's Right".

This is a site people can visit and get a better understanding of the community work going on in Louisville, Kentucky. People are welcome to view, discuss, and possibly become involved with some of the major contributors helping to make this fight even. We will have highlights, spotlights, good light, and some bad light, but it will be all encompassing of the work being done, the work's consequences, and stories of how everyday, ordinary people are paving the way for freedom, justice, equality, and overall change in their communities and beyond.

If you would like to follow anyone we discuss on Twitter, please see the Contacts Page for their username. For Facebook, please also see our Contacts Page for a link to their page(s).
